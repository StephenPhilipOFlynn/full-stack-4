Our Story

Welcome to Leticia's, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of Dublin City, where the vibrant flavors of Brazil and Spain are brought to life. Our story begins with Leticia, our founder and head chef, whose childhood in São Paulo and sun-kissed holidays in Southern Brazil ignited her love for Brazilian cuisine. This love was further enriched by her family's Spanish grandparents, whose stories and recipes from Spain add a unique Galician flair to her cooking. Leticia's journey is a tale of two worlds, beautifully intertwined on a plate. Our dishes showcase the best of Brazilian and Spanish gastronomy. From the succulent churrasco and feijoada of Brazil to the tantalising tapas and paellas of Spain, every dish is crafted with the finest ingredients and an unwavering commitment to authenticity and quality. Everyone is welcome, and we are here to introduce the uninitiated to the exquisite flavours of these rich culinary traditions, with our menu that is at once familiar and adventurous. Every meal at Leticia's is a celebration of life, love, and the unifying power of great food.
